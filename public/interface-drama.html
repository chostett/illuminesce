The Interface Drama Master List

Interface dramas are an emergent genre of games that use software to tell interactive stories. They could use chats, emails, blog posts, or even interfaces that don't exist yet. Our game, Terranova, is an interface drama. This master list is for people wanting to play and discover more games in this unique genre.

Check out the most recent tag below for the most recent additions. Last updated May 3rd, 2025.
