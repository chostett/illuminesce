Recipes

My approach to food can be summed up by Michael Pollan's quote: "Eat food. Not too much. Mostly plants."

Food is meant to be shared and enjoyed with friends and family. Since many of my friends are vegetarian or vegan, I like cooking veggies. I'm gluten-intolerant, so most of my recipes tend to be gluten-free.
