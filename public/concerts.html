concerts

I love music and going to concerts. It's one of the ways I experience collective effervescence. In particular, I like seeing Japanese visual kei and rock bands in concert.

I have been extremely lucky to have lived in places with thriving music scenes (Austin and Tokyo) and have friends that love concerts, too. Below are a list of some of the concerts I've attended over the years.
