interaction 16 in Helsinki, Finland

Designing for Play: What Interaction Design Can Learn From Video Games

We were video game fanatics before we were interaction designers. Years later we can’t stop seeing the many parallels between interaction design and video game design. In fact we keep seeing the future of interaction design coming closer and closer to the field of video games. We see the future of interfaces that allow us to play, discover, and connect to people in a positive and wonderful way.
