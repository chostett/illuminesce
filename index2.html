こんにちは and howdy, y’all.

I’m CJ, a Texan in Tokyo. I’m a digital designer and researcher who makes software, apps, and video games.


In 2022, together with mabbees, we released Terranova, a game set in the early 2000's following four fangirls and their roleplay community online.

I am an experienced speaker in English and Japanese, and have given talks worldwide on design, video games and advocacy in the LGBTQ+ community. I use they/them pronouns.
