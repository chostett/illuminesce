IWAKAN Volume 02 特集 愛情

The Love Issue

■English summary | 英語の概要

Questioning stereotypes surrounding love.

We, who are discontent with the rules and stereotypes of love created by society and how love should be, regardless of gender. These stereotypes include, "the goal of love is to be married," or "Happiness is to love and be loved by others," or "love should be single-minded and unchanging," or "only love that can be proven is "right..."

Love is supposed to be free. Why are we bound by so many rules?

In this issue, we question the stereotypes around love from different angles. This issue includes an interview with Mitsu Tachibana, owner and entertainer in a lesbian sex club, a "divorce memoir" that catalogues the experiences of divorced women from different countries and races, and a column on Adachi Ward, which has announced plans to establish a partnership system and more.

■日本語の原板 | Original Japanese (via the online store)

愛をとりまく固定観念を問い直す

社会が作り上げた恋愛のルールへの違和や、ジェンダーやバイナリーにとらわれない愛のあり方。恋愛のゴールは結婚、他者に愛し愛されることが幸福、愛は一途で不変であるべき、証明できる愛こそが正しい...。愛は自由なはずなのに、何故こんなにも多くのルールに縛られなくてはいけないのか？ 愛をとりまく固定観念に、様々な角度から“違和感”を問いかける一冊。“対話型レズ風俗“のオーナー兼キャストの橘みつのインタビューをはじめ、国も人種も異なる母親たちの離婚経験を記録した「離婚回顧録」、パートナーシップ制度に関する要綱を制定予定と発表した足立区に関するコラム、アートワークページ「違和感瞬間ー泡沫の縁」などを収録。
