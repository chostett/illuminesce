未来の男性へーIWAKAN書簡集

Dear Future Men: Letters from IWAKAN

■English summary | 英語の概要

37 letters to the men of the future.

This is a collection of essays to shed light on the darkness of "masculinity" that we face, in conjunction with "IWAKAN Volume 06: The Masculinity Issue. Many of the images of masculinity that emerged from the readers' questionnaires sent to the editorial office were dark and hopeless. Therefore, we called on activists, journalists, creators, and scholars with close ties to IWAKAN to collect 37 letters in order to think together about a healthier future in which men can be freer.

CJ Note: This is a labor of love and obsession with documenting queer and alternate voices in Japan. If you are a translator/native speaker and feel I've made an error, please contact me at (illuminesce @ pm (.) me).

■日本語の原板 | Original Japanese (via the online store)

未来の男性たちへ向けた、37通の手紙

『IWAKAN Volume 06 特集男性制』に連動する、私たちが直面した「男らしさ」の闇に光を照らすためのエッセイ集。編集部に寄せられた読者アンケートから見えてきた男性性のイメージは、暗く哀れな印象のものが多く見受けられた。そこで、IWAKANにゆかりの深いアクティビスト、ジャーナリスト、クリエイター、学者らに呼びかけ、男性がもう少し自由になれるような、より健全な未来を一緒に考えるための37通の手紙を集めた書簡集。
