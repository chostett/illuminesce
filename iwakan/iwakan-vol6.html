IWAKAN Volume 06 特集 男性制

The Masculinity Issue

■English summary | 英語の概要

Free yourself from the rusted concept of "masculinity."

We want to bring awareness to binary thinking and patriarchy that produced it, and to free ourselves from this rusty, fixed image and pressure of "masculinity." Masculinity is not only for those with penises.

This issue explores a new masculinity in an evolving age and society. It includes interviews with Kai Nishii, known for his book "Pity for Men: the Agonies and Contradictions of Unpopular Boys", and Kabuki actor Chinnosuke Kataoka, who has taken on both the tachiyaku (male) and onnagata (female) roles in kabuki, as well as the photo series "A moment of discomfort - Men" about an office worker who loses consciousness after drinking too much of the patriarchy.

The magazine's first bilingual edition in English and Japanese.

CJ Note: This is a labor of love and obsession with documenting queer and alternate voices in Japan. If you are a translator/native speaker and feel I've made an error, please contact me at (illuminesce @ pm (.) me).

■日本語の原板 | Original Japanese (via the online store)

錆びついた「男らしさ」から解き放たれよ

男女二元論や家父長制に基づく「男らしさ」の存在に意識を向け、錆びつき、固定化されたイメージやプレッシャーから解き放たれるために。男性性はペニスを持つ者だけのものではない、進化する時代と社会の中で新たな男性性を探求する一冊。『「非モテ」からはじめる男性学』で知られる西井開や立役と女形の両方に挑戦する歌舞伎役者・片岡千之助へのインタビュー、「家父長制」を飲み過ぎて意識を失ってしまったサラリーマンを描いた「違和感瞬間ー男」など。マガジン初の日英バイリンガルでお届け。
